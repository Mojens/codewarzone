About

Who am I?

Hello! My name is Mohammad, and welcome to my website, where aspiring programmers can practice and hone their skills in various programming languages.

As a student at KEA (Copenhagen School of Design and Technology), I have a strong passion for programming and a deep understanding of the challenges faced by students learning to code. This led me to work as a programming tutor, helping others navigate the complex world of JavaScript, Java, Spring, MySQL, Maven, and more.

I am currently expanding my expertise by learning Python, Swift, and Android development, and I have a vision for this website to eventually support exercises for all of these languages.

My goal is to make learning different programming languages enjoyable and accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or experience level.

What to Expect

  • A wide range of exercises: You'll find exercises that cover fundamental concepts and more advanced topics for various programming languages, giving you the opportunity to grow and challenge yourself at your own pace.
  • Step-by-step solutions: For each exercise, I provide a detailed solution, complete with explanations and best practices. This way, you can learn from your mistakes and deepen your understanding of the programming languages.
  • A supportive community: I encourage you to share your experiences, ask questions, and engage with others on this journey. Together, we can learn and grow as programmers.
  • Regular updates: I continuously add new exercises and content to keep the learning experience fresh and up-to-date with the latest trends in software development.

As the website evolves, I will be adding exercises and resources for Python, Swift, and Java, making it a comprehensive platform for learning multiple programming languages.

I hope this website helps you become a confident and skilled programmer in the languages of your choice. If you have any suggestions or feedback, please feel free to reach out to me.

Let's learn and grow together!

Happy coding!
